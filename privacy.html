Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how the Pipact application uses information.

Data Use

  • No personal data is stored permanently by the service.
  • No data is sold or shared with third parties.
  • Authentication and any payments are handled through Pi Network.
  • Information is used only to operate and maintain the application.

Retention

Transient operational data may be processed to provide the service but is not retained beyond what is necessary to support the app.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the Pipact maintainers through the project repository.
